Building Community Through Yoga

5800 Yoga is a place where likeminded yoga enthusiests can come together and practice yoga together. We offer yoga classes in unique locations all throughout the denver metro area. We also offer two retriets per year where we fly out to exotic locations like Bali and Tulum where you meet with experienced yoga instructors to further your yoga practice in a beautiful location. Whether you are a beginner or have been practicing for years 5800 yoga is the place for you!
